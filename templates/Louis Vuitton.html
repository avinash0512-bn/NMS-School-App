Why Louis Vuitton ?

You really don't have to worry about any kind of damage, if you are the lucky owner of a canvas Louis V. bag. The canvas was originally developed for travel gear, like suitcases, so it's a really sturdy material. Don't fear for any scratching as this type of canvas is indestructible. You can easily toss your bag around, no biggie.

The Neverfull bag is one of Louis Vuitton’s most popular bags. The chic, yet extremely practical tote, was released in 2007 and has since then become a staple in any luxury handbag lovers collection. More so, many Louis Vuitton fans and handbag enthusiasts tend to own the Neverfull in a variety of the available colours and sizes.
